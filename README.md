## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the sales performance of **Vilvah Store** over a period of **10 years (2015–2025)**.  
The goal is to understand **customer behavior, product performance, and business trends**.

## 📊 Insights

- 📌 Sales are evenly distributed between male and female customers
- 📌 Serum category has the highest sales contribution
- 📌 Sales peak during July to September months
- 📌 Yearly sales show stable and consistent growth
- 📌 All cities perform similarly with minimal variation
